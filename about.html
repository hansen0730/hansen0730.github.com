学习 经历

I have been on both the agency and the client-side, and I've been lucky to have worked with some great companies and clients.

I currently work for an interactive agency called Nurun, as a Senior User Experience Architect, where I work with Nespresso, GE Energy, The Home Depot among others.

Previously, I have worked with Coca Cola, L'Oreal, Verizon, and Cartoon Network. I have also worked at places like Microsoft, and Lockheed Martin.

I have a Masters of Human-Computer Interaction from Carnegie Mellon, and have undergraduate degrees in Psychology and in Computer Science, focusing on game development, from UMBC.
